Tramway for the city of Regensburg (2022)

Design can create a balance between vehicle, architectural elements and the urban environment.
We gathered impressions from Ratisbona and were inspired by the cityscape.
Various typical buildings and their materiality led us to the frame construction inside the Parametric Tram.

The projects results were displayed from 15th July to 26th July 2022

Winter semester 21/22 - Prof Jakob Timpe
Project at the OTH Regensburg together with Valentin Albiker
